Welcome to Coding With Kids’s documentation!

Coding with kids can be a great way to not only teach them some skills that will help them with their own lives, but also create or strengthen some lasting bonds between you and them.

Throught your journey you will both likely learn new concepts and terms that will expand your horizons and provide hours of enjoyment.

Note

Some kids have problems with screen time and if you leave them unattended with a screen and they have access to thigs like Youtube or games, they may not stay focused on the task and hand.
